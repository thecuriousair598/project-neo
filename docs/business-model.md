# Business Model & Revenue Streams

## Executive Summary

Project Neo operates on a **multi-tiered freemium SaaS model** designed to maximize market reach while building sustainable recurring revenue.

**Revenue Vision:**
- **Year 1-2**: Prove product-market fit, build customer base
- **Year 3-5**: Scale enterprise adoption
- **Year 5+**: Platform economics and ecosystem revenue

---

## Pricing Tiers

### **Free Tier**
- Basic automation capabilities
- Limited premium features
- Community support
- **Purpose:** Viral marketing, user acquisition

### **Trial Tier**
- Full feature access
- Time-limited evaluation
- Support during trial
- **Purpose:** Convert teams to paid plans

### **Tier 1: Starter ($10-30/user/month)**
- Core AI automation
- Essential integrations
- Standard support
- **Add-ons:** Connectors, processing, support

### **Tier 2: Professional ($30-100/user/month)**
- Advanced automation
- Expanded integrations
- Team collaboration
- Analytics and insights
- **Add-ons:** Premium connectors, custom integrations

### **Tier 3: Enterprise ($100-500+/user/month)**
- Comprehensive integrations
- Enterprise security and compliance
- White-label options
- Custom AI capabilities
- Premium SLAs
- **Add-ons:** Custom development, professional services

---

## Revenue Streams

### **1. Subscription Revenue** (Primary)
- Recurring monthly/annual subscriptions
- Tiered pricing by features and scale
- Volume discounts and multi-year contracts

### **2. Usage-Based Revenue**
- AI API consumption
- Document processing
- Premium connectors

### **3. Professional Services**
- Implementation and onboarding
- Custom development
- Training and consulting

### **4. Partner Ecosystem**
- System integrator partnerships
- Technology partner programs
- Connector marketplace

### **5. Data & Insights** (Future)
- Industry benchmarking
- Best practice reports

### **6. Platform Revenue** (Level 2+)
- Custom app generation
- Workflow marketplace
